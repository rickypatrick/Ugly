Homepage Redesign

Navigation

UX studies indicate that website navigations with more than seven navigation links become confusing to the end-user. This website has 12 top-level navigation links, including the Facebook and Cart icons.

Removing the logo, which clashes with the rest of the design, frees up space in the navigation. Trimming back the number of top-level navigation items also leaves room to add the phone number and address. Due to the nature of a business like this, quick access to location and contact info is paramount. The yellow is used as an accent color for the phone number, the active state on the navigation item, and the sub-level navigation items.
